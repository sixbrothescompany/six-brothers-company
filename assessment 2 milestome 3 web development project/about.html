Introduction

The Six Brothers Company is a private enterprise focused on driving sustainable development through investments in agriculture and real estate sectors. Our goal is to drive economic growth and support community welfare.

Teams

The success of any project relies on the effective collaboration and clear understanding of roles and responsibilities. The following are the key roles and responsibilities for the project:

  • Director: Overall project oversight and decision-making.
  • Project Manager: Day-to-day management, coordination, and reporting.
  • Agriculture Team: Responsible for planning and executing agriculture-related activities.
  • Real Estate Team: Responsible for planning and executing real estate-related activities.
  • Community Welfare Team: Responsible for planning and executing community support initiatives.
  • Finance Team: Budget management and financial reporting.
  • Local Stakeholders: Collaboration and support for community engagement.

Project Notes

Project notes serve as a record of discussions, decisions, and actions taken throughout the project. It is essential to document project notes regularly to maintain a comprehensive record and facilitate smooth collaboration among team members.

  • Documentation Responsibility: The Project Manager
  • Format: Digital Document stored on a Shared Drive (Google Drive) and Hard copy secure lock at office.
  • Frequency: Updated after each key meeting and reviewed fortnightly for accuracy and progress tracking.
